Grilled Cheese

Description

Experience comfort on a plate with our classic grilled cheese recipe. Simple, savory, and satisfyingly gooey, it's the ultimate comfort food for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white, wheat, or sourdough)
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, Swiss, or your preferred cheese)
  • Butter or margarine, softened

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet or frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Spread butter or margarine evenly on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, on the skillet.
  4. Layer the cheese slices on top of the bread in the skillet.
  5. Place the second slice of bread, buttered side up, on top of the cheese.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown and crispy.
  7. Carefully flip the sandwich using a spatula.
  8. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the second side is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  9. Remove the grilled cheese from the skillet and let it cool for a minute.
  10. Cut the sandwich diagonally, if desired, and serve hot. Enjoy your delicious grilled cheese sandwich!
